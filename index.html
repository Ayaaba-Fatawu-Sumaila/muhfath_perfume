About Abdul Muhaimin

Crafting Scents That Tell Your Story

MuhFath is a startup perfume company located in Kumasi Suame. Our journey began in the heart of Kumasi, where we discovered the art of perfumery through generations of tradition and craftsmanship.

Each MuhFath perfume scent is meticulously crafted using the finest ingredients sourced from around the globe. Our master perfumers blend traditional techniques with modern innovation to create unique, long-lasting fragrances that evoke emotion and create lasting memories.

We believe that perfume is more than just a scent - it's an extension of your personality, a memory captured in a bottle, and a form of self-expression. That's why we pour our hearts into every bottle we create to help you feel confident and radiant with our glowing perfumes.
